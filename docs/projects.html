Single Channel Sim

This is an interactive web-based Monte-Carlo simulator for single channel ion currents, for learning ion channel electrophysiology. It allows users to configure various channel parameters (conductance, voltage, duration, noise) and visualizes the resulting single-channel current traces through multiple interactive charts of mean traces, Alvarez plots, and coefficient of variation analysis. The simulator is designed to help students understand ion channel behavior by modeling realistic electrophysiological data and fitting mathematical models to the results.
